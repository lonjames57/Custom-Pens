Ordering your custom pen is very easy - as easy as 1, 2,3!

1. Please read the "About Me" page as it has important information about pen creation and shipping details.

2. Pick ONE of the FIVE Pen Styles on the "Choose Pen STYLE" tab. This will automatically take you to the final screen.

3. Here you can pick your desired metal components and the barrel body of wood or acrylic finish. You may make changes on this final screen and see the real-time results at the top. When you are satisfied with your selections, hit the green "Send to Cart" button.

Fill in the cart information with payment and shipping information and that's it!
