I have been involved in computer graphics for most of my life. I enjoy making designs that are pleasing and that has spurred my love for making pens.The patterns I see as each pen takes shape are fun and interesting. I don't just want to make pens, I want to make pens that people want!

I make each pen by hand, according to your specifications. It's really quite easy. You choose the STYLE of pen you desire, your choice of metal finish, and if you want a wood or acrylic body. I take care of everything else.

Please note that I am NOT a warehouse or supplier, so I have to order the supplies for every pen. That means in addition to my time of actually making the pen, I have to wait for supplies to be shipped to me. This means I cannot make any "expedited" or emergency pens.

You should allow two weeks from the order time for completion of your pen. I will usually be done well before that but I have no control over other companies shipping policies.

I promise to keep you appraised of any delays or problems.
